Baked Sweet Potato

Ingredients

  • Organic Sweet Potato

Instructions

  1. Wash and slice lengthwise.
  2. Lay flat (skin up) on baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Bake at 400°F for 30 minutes.
Baked Russet Potato

Ingredients

  • Small to medium sized russet potatoes.

Instructions

  1. Poke potatoes with fork on both sides.
  2. Bake at 400°F for 25 minutes.
  3. Turn potatoes over and bake for another 25 minutes.
Beans - Instant Pot - Rancho Gorgo

Instructions

  1. Rinse/wash beans
  2. Soak beans for 12 - 24 hours in filtered water
  3. Do not drain. Rather, cook with soaking water.
  4. Just cover (⅛” above) the beans with veggie broth.
  5. Use manual high pressure.
  6. 6-20 min for soaked. 20-40 min for unsoaked.
  7. Natural pressure release. 20+ minutes.

Cold Brew Coffee

Ingredients

  • Cold, filtered water.
  • Your favorite coffee (Philz Coffee). I’ve read that lighter roasts are best (will test later).

Ratio (Same as pour over)

  • 2 g coffee to 1 oz Water
  • 24 g coffee to 12 oz water
  • 32 g coffee to 16 oz water
  • 64 g coffee to 32 oz water
  • 96 g coffee to 48 oz (6 cups) water. Yields 40 oz.
  • 112 g coffee to 56 oz (7 cups) water. Yields 48 oz or 3, 16 oz glasses.
  • 128 g coffee to 64 oz (8 cups) water
  • 144 g coffee to 72 oz (9 cups) water
  • 160 g coffee to 80 oz (10 cups) water

Instructions

  1. Set burr grinder (Capresso) to medium range, grind coffee.
  2. Courser Grind = smoother, lighter, clearer, longer brew time.
  3. Finer Grind = stronger, thicker, increased filtering time, cloudier, shorter brew time.
  4. Add to a large glass pitcher, pour the water on top and stir.
  5. Brew times
    • Room temperature: 14 – 18 hrs. I may have been over-brewing into bitter territory.
    • Refrigerator: 20 – 24 hrs.
  6. Stir one more time.
  7. Filter through a paper filter. Chemex Bonded Filters & Chemex Caraf.
  8. Add water and or milk to taste. I use Oatly, Regular Oat Milk or Califia Unsweetened Almond Milk. Enjoy!
